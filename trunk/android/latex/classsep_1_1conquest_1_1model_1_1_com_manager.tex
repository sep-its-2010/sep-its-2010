\subsection{Detailed Description}
The \hyperlink{classsep_1_1conquest_1_1model_1_1_com_manager}{ComManager} class is the global communication-\/manager to provide broadcast-\/communication. It has a (thread-\/safe) list with all clients who are registered. Every registered participant will get the request-\/messages from others. Classes which have implemented the IComClient-\/interface can be registered by the addClient-\/method. Requests will be sent by the broadcast-\/ method.
